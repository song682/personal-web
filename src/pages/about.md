## About Me

Hello there! I'm Seniye, an ordinary person who loves life and has all sorts of quirky little hobbies.

In my daily life, what I enjoy most is spending time with cats. Their soft fur and lazy demeanor always heal all my weariness. I believe every cat has its own unique personality, just like every person.

Translation is another passion of mine. Through the conversion of text, I can feel the collision and fusion of different cultures, which makes the world feel smaller and more interesting to me. Whether it's novels, articles, or simple dialogues, the process of translation is a joy for me.

Besides these, I also love exploring the corners of the city in my spare time, discovering those little hidden beauties unknown to many; I also enjoy trying my hand at making various delicious foods. Although I occasionally fail, the process is always full of fun.

This little website is like a secret base for me, recording some of my thoughts and daily life. I'm glad you found your way here, and I hope you can feel this simple joy too.
